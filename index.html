Salar Jung Museum

The must see exhibits includes Quran collections, Clocks, Coins, Lakshmi Narayan The deity Lakshmi Narayan has many historic roots and is sometimes interpreted differently by different traditions. Goddess Lakshmi incarnated again and again with Her beloved consort following Narayana's wish and mode of incarnation Anantashayana Vishnu The image was carved during the first quarter of the 9th century CE when the Bhauma-Kara that ruled in central part of Orissa.

Section with an ordered and unordered lists

Salar Jung Museum is located 24 kilometres from Rajiv Gandhi International Airport. There are 3 best posiible ways by car. This is the fastest way

  1. Airport Approach Road, NH765 and Kandikal Gate Rd to Gowlipura Rd in Chatrinaka
    1. At the roundabout, take the 3rd exit and stay on Airport Approach Road
    2. Turn left onto NH765
    3. Slight left onto Barkas Rd/Srisailam Hwy
    4. Turn left after Vijaya Diagnostic Centre Chandrayangutta (on the left)
  2. Gowlipura Rd to Sultan Shahi Rd in Sultan Shahi
    1. Turn right onto Gowlipura Rd
    2. Turn right toward Sultan Shahi Rd
    3. Turn left to stay on Sultan Shahi Rd
  3. Sultan Shahi Rd and Mir Alam Mandi Rd to Darulshifa
    1. Turn right after Meherban Hotel (on the right)
    2. At New King's Safina Hotel, continue onto Mir Alam Mandi Rd
    3. Turn left toward Salar Jung Rd
  4. Turn left toward Salar Jung Road
    1. Continue onto Salar Jung Rd
